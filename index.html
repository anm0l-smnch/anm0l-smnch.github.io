Anmol Somanchi!

About me

I am currently a Research Fellow in the Economics Group at the Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad. I have completed a Bachelors in Economics from St. Stephen's College, Delhi and a Masters in Development Studies from Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai. I then spent two years as an Associate at IDinsight in New Delhi. My current research focuses on the the status of food and social security in India, governance reform - particularly the role of Aadhaar in India's welfare programs, and the potential of education technology in the Indian context.
